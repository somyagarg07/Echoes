TEXT-TO-SPEECH


Our website now includes a state-of-the-art text-to-speech feature, designed to enhance accessibility and user experience. This tool allows users to listen to the content on our site, making it easier for those with visual impairments or reading difficulties to access information. Simply click the play button next to any text, and our advanced speech synthesis technology will read it aloud in a clear and natural voice. Whether you're multitasking, on the go, or just prefer listening over reading, our text-to-speech feature ensures you can stay informed and engaged with ease.

Speech-to-text


Our website now features an advanced speech-to-text tool, designed to enhance accessibility and user interaction. This feature allows users to convert spoken words into written text effortlessly, making it easier for those with disabilities or those who prefer speaking over typing to engage with our content. Simply click the microphone icon, speak into your device, and watch as your words are transcribed in real-time. Whether you're dictating notes, composing messages, or navigating the site, our speech-to-text feature ensures a seamless and inclusive experience for all users.
